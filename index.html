Race day tactics
Positioning
Stay top third of the field — never the back. The accordion effect at the back costs huge energy. Move up on the straights, not through corners.
Cornering
Brake before the corner, not in it. Carry speed through and accelerate out. On a rectangle course you'll do this hundreds of times — smooth is fast.
Pacing
Don't go all-in on lap one. Settle in, find a wheel, and save something for the final 5–10 min. The race is decided in the last 2–3 laps.
Wind strategy
At 206 lbs you push more air than lighter riders. Stay sheltered on headwind straights — sit in the bunch and let others do the work.
The finish
Start moving up with 2 laps to go. The final sprint in a crit starts earlier than people expect. Don't get boxed in — move to the outside in the last lap.
Pre-race week
Aug 12–14: add 50–75g extra carbs/day. Reduce fiber the day before. No alcohol that week. Last gym session Aug 12 (Tuesday activation only).
